Interior Design is the art of creating a beautiful balance between functionality and aesthetics and creating harmony between human life and its environment.

About us

Elliott-Harrison specialises in transforming your rooms into a place you can call home. Interior Design is all about creating somewhere that reflects your “soul” and resonates with you as an individual. We believe in “affordable luxury” and will work within your budget and your timeline to create a home that you can be proud of. More importantly, our passion is creating a space you cannot wait to come home to and having fun along the way.

Jackie Elliott-Harrison

FOUNDER

Jackie has worked in this industry for over 22 years and prides herself on her exceptional level of service and warm professionalism. Most importantly, she listens to her clients and finds solutions to fit expectations. Jackie has experience in all aspects of the industry, having had her own manufacturing workroom for many years, and having represented Warwick Fabrics as International Brand Manager for several years. Jackie also sold textiles into some of the biggest Textile and Interior Companies in the USA. She also has experience in the lighting industry and production of textiles. As a natural progression, Elliott-Harrison Design came into fruition allowing Jackie to combine her many years of experience and passion for interior design.
